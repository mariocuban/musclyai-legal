Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our facial analysis service:

  • Photos: Facial photos you upload for analysis. These are processed to generate your personalized improvement plan.
  • Profile Information: Gender, age range, ethnicity, location, and preferences you provide during onboarding.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how you use the app, including screen views and feature usage.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status managed through Apple's App Store. We do not store your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Analyze your facial features and generate personalized recommendations.
  • Generate simulated "after" images showing potential improvements.
  • Customize the app experience based on your profile and preferences.
  • Improve our analysis algorithms and app features.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Photos are stored locally on your device and transmitted securely (via HTTPS) to our analysis servers only during active analysis sessions.
  • We do not permanently store your facial photos on our servers after analysis is complete.
  • Profile data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms.
  • We use industry-standard encryption to protect data in transit.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For facial feature analysis (photos are sent securely and not retained by OpenAI per their API data usage policy).
  • Image Processing APIs: For generating simulated improvement images.
  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions. RevenueCat's privacy policy applies to subscription data.
  • Apple Sign In: For authentication. Apple's privacy policy applies.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data: Use "Log Out" in Settings to remove all locally stored data.
  • Redo onboarding: Reset your profile information at any time.
  • Request data deletion: Contact us at support@musclyai.com to request removal of any server-side data.
  • Opt out: You can stop using the app at any time and your local data will be removed upon logout.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
